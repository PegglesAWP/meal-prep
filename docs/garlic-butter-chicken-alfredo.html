Ingredients

Garlic Chicken

  • 900g (32oz) chicken breast
  • 30g (¼ cup) Parmigiano Reggiano Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2Tbsp garlic purée
  • 2tsp garlic powder
  • 2tsp onion powder
  • 1Tbsp salt
  • 1tsp black pepper
  • 2tsp Kinder’s “brown butter roasted garlic” seasoning Dash of crushed red pepper
  • 120g (½ cup) water
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter

Blended Alfredo Sauce

  • 800g (3 ⅓ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 120g (½ cup) 1/3-fat cream cheese
  • 120g (1 cup) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 240g (1 cup) 1% milk

Pasta

  • 120g (½ cup) pasta water the chicken is tender and shreds easily.

Instructions

  1. Make the Garlic chicken: Dice the chicken breasts and grate the Parmigiano Reggiano. Add to the slow cooker with lemon juice, garlic purée, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, crushed red pepper, Kinder’s “brown butter roasted garlic” seasoning and water.   5. Divide into 10 servings and store leftovers in airtight containers in the freezer for easy meal prep.
