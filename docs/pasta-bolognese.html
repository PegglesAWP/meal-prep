Pasta Bolognese

Ingredients

Beef & Seasonings

  • 1340g (48oz) 93% ground beef
  • 5 slices center-cut bacon
  • 1580g (56 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2Tbsp each: oregano, salt
  • 1Tbsp each: dried thyme, red pepper flakes, black pepper
  • 0.5tsp cayenne
  • 0.25tsp nutmeg

Pasta

  • 672g (24oz) pasta of choice

Sauce Base

  • 45g (3 Tbsp) Butter
  • 3 onions
  • 3cloves of garlic
  • 90g (6 Tbsp) Tomato Paste
  • 454g (16oz) beef bone broth
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) Worcestershire
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) soy sauce

Toppings & Garnish

  • 360g (1 ½ cups) fat-free evaporated milk
  • 100g (1 cup) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 30g (1½ Tbsp) honey
  • 4 dried bay leaves Store leftovers in freezer-safe containers.

Instructions

  1. Dice the onions and mince the garlic cloves. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the onions until translucent and just beginning to brown. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Add the butter, garlic, and tomato paste, stir to combine. Lower the heat to medium, simmer for 7-8 minutes.
  2. Turn the heat to high and add beef bone broth, Worcestershire sauce, and soy sauce. Stir well, scraping the bottom of the skillet to deglaze. Boil until liquid is completely reduced.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Add ground beef, crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried thyme, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper, cayenne, and nutmeg. Mix to ensure the seasonings are evenly distributed. Top with bay leaves. Dice center-cut bacon and line the edges of the slow cooker with the bacon, or cook the bacon separately on the stove and add at the end. Slow cook on low for 5-6 hours or high for 3-4 hours.
  4. Once slow cooker is finished, remove bay leaves. Shred Parmigiano Reggiano and mix in the slow cooker along with fat-free evaporated milk, and honey until smooth. Cover while you prep pasta.
  5. Cook pasta for half the time listed on the package, then drain.
  6. Break up the meat, then add half-cooked pasta, mix, and cover. Cook on high for 20-30 minutes to finish cooking the pasta and thicken the sauce. Divide into 12 servings, adding a sprinkle of Parmigiano Reggiano on each.
