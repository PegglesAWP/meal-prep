Ingredients

Garlic Rosemary Chicken

  • 1360g (48oz) chicken thighs
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) Worcestershire
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) minced garlic
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) Dijon mustard
  • 1Tbsp fine grain salt
  • 2tsp black pepper
  • 1tsp dried thyme
  • 100g (½ cup) bone broth Rosemary sprig
  • 1Tbsp garlic powder
  • 2tsp onion powder

Pasta

  • 672g (24 oz) pasta of choice
  • Garnish: Chopped chives

Caramelized Onions

  • 4 diced yellow onions
  • 960g (32oz) beef bone broth
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) soy sauce
  • 5g (1 tsp) Dijon mustard
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) Worcestershire Optional: rosemary sprig & thyme

Cheese Sauce

  • 800g (3 ⅓ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 200g (~7 oz) Gruyère
  • 75g (2 ½ oz) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 380g (1 ½ cups) 1% milk To taste: black pepper/salt sauce thickens.

Instructions

  1. Mince the garlic and place in the slow cooker with the chicken thighs, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme, black pepper, and bone broth. Stir to coat, add a rosemary sprig on top, cover, and cook on high for 3-4 hrs or low for 4-5 hrs until tender.
  2. Meanwhile, dice onions and, add to a pan over medium high heat with salt and butter, stirring until deeply browned, about 30-45 minutes. Add beef bone broth, Worcestershire, soy sauce, Dijon mustard, and optionally add a sprig of rosemary and thyme. Cook until liquid evaporates and onions are jammy. Set aside.
  3. Shred Gruyère and Parmigiano Reggiano and place in a blender along with the 2% cottage cheese, 1% milk, black pepper, salt, and 3/4 of the caramelized onions until smooth.
  4. When the chicken is done, shred lightly in the slow cooker. Cook pasta halfway, drain, and add with the cheese sauce to the slow cooker. Stir to combine. Cover and cook on low for 15-30 minutes until pasta is tender and
  5. Add remaining caramelized onions and chopped chives, and mix. Portion into 12 servings. Enjoy!
