Ingredients

Chicken

  • 900g (32oz) chicken breast

Yogurt & Butter

  • 250g (1 cup) plain 0% Greek yogurt or Skyr
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter

Optional

  • 15g (1 Tbsp) chicken bouillon
  • 1Tbsp sugar or zero calorie sweetener

Butter Sauce

  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter
  • 2 onions
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) tomato paste
  • 2Tbsp garlic paste
  • 2Tbsp ginger paste
  • 2Tbsp paprika
  • 2Tbsp garam masala
  • 1tsp cayenne
  • 1tsp cumin
  • 1tsp coriander
  • 2tsp salt
  • 1 (14oz) can crushed fireroasted tomatoes Dash of black pepper well to combine. tender and cooked through.

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet on medium heat. Chop onions, and add. Cook until caramelized.
  2. Stir in tomato paste, garlic paste, and ginger paste. Cook for 2-3 min until fragrant, then add the seasoning blend (paprika, cayenne, garam masala, cumin, coriander, salt, and black pepper). Stir
  3. Pour in fire-roasted tomatoes and splash of water, stir to deglaze pan. Simmer for 2-3 min.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a blender. For optional flavor depth, add chicken bouillon and sugar or sweetener. Blend for about 2 minutes until the sauce is completely smooth.
  5. Dice the chicken breast and place it in the slow cooker. Pour the butter sauce over the chicken, ensuring it is fully coated. Cover and cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is
  6. Once cooked, add additional butter and 0% Greek yogurt to the slow cooker. Stir until well combined. Cover and let sit for another 10-15 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  7. Divide into 8 servings and store leftovers in freezer-safe containers for meal prep.
