Ingredients

Chicken & Marinade

  • 1340g (48oz) boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 60g (2 packets) taco seasoning
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) lime juice
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) chicken bouillon
  • 420g (15oz) green enchilada sauce To taste: garlic powder, salt

Pico de Gallo

  • 5 diced Roma tomatoes
  • 3 jalapeños
  • 2 white onions
  • 1bunch chopped cilantro Juice of 2 limes

Rice

  • 400g (2 cups) (uncooked weight) of any rice

Cheesy Mix

  • 500g (1 ¾ cups) 0% Greek yogurt
  • 150g (1 ½ cups) reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  • 120g (1 cup) reduced-fat mozzarella cheese

Tortillas

  • 18 Burrito-sized Tortillas mixture. Let cool to thicken. freeze.

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken thighs into the slow cooker. Add taco seasoning, lime juice, chicken bouillon, green enchilada sauce, garlic, and salt to taste. Stir to coat evenly. Cover and cook on high for 2–3 hours or low for 3–4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easy to shred.

  3. Cook rice according to packaging instructions.
