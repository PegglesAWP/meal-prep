Slow Cooker Beef, Bean & Lentil Ragu

Ingredients

Beef

  • 1000g beef chuck or brisket, cut into large chunks
  • 1Tbsp olive oil
  • salt and black pepper

Aromatics

  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 2 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2Tbsp tomato paste

Sauce & Legumes

  • 800g (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 400g (14oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 200g (1 cup) dried brown or green lentils, rinsed
  • 400g (14oz) can cannellini or kidney beans, drained
  • 250ml (1 cup) beef stock
  • 125ml (1/2 cup) red wine (optional)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1tsp dried oregano
  • 1tsp dried thyme
  • 0.5tsp chilli flakes (optional)
  • 1Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large pan over high heat. Sear beef on all sides until well browned (3-4 minutes per side). Transfer to slow cooker.
  2. In the same pan, sauté onion, carrot, and celery over medium heat for 5 minutes until softened. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook 2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Deglaze with red wine (if using), scraping up browned bits. Pour into slow cooker.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, lentils, beef stock, bay leaves, oregano, thyme, chilli flakes, and Worcestershire sauce. Stir to combine.
  5. Cover and cook on LOW for 6-8 hours or HIGH for 3-4 hours, until beef is falling apart and lentils are tender.
  6. Remove beef, shred with two forks, return to slow cooker. Add drained beans and stir. Cook 15-20 minutes more.
  7. Remove bay leaves. Season to taste. Serve over pasta, rice, or with crusty bread. Top with parmesan and fresh herbs.
  8. Divide into 10 portions and store in freezer-safe containers. Keeps 4 days in fridge, 3 months in freezer.
