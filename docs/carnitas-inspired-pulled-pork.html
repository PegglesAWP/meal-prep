Ingredients

Meat

  • 1790g (64 oz) pork loin

Marinade

  • 2 onions
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) oil of choice
  • 2Tbsp salt
  • 1Tbsp oregano
  • 2tsp black pepper
  • 1tsp cumin Juice from 2 oranges
  • 6cloves garlic
  • 0.25tsp cinnamon
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) apple cider vinegar
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 40g (2 Tbsp) honey achieve the desired consistency. containers for meal prep.

Cornstarch Slurry (if needed)

  • 5g (1 tsp) cornstarch
  • 1tsp water

Instructions

  1. Cut a pork loin into 4-5 large chunks and set aside. Dice the onions and cook in a skillet over medium-high heat with oil until browned and caramelized, about 8-10 min. Stir to avoid burning.
  2. Place pork and caramelized onions in the slow cooker. Add salt, black pepper, oregano, cumin, cinnamon, garlic cloves, honey, and apple cider vinegar. Squeeze the juice from the oranges over the mix, then place the orange peels over the meat in the slow cooker. Add the bay leaves.
  3. Cover and cook on high for 4-5 hours or low for 5-6 hours, until the pork is tender and easily shredded. Once the pork is cooked, remove all liquid from the slow cooker and transfer it to a pan and remove bay leaves. Boil the liquid over medium-high heat to reduce for 10-15 minutes or until slightly thickened.
