Ingredients

Chicken & Sauce Base

  • 900g (32oz) chicken breast
  • 2 onions
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) tomato paste
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) garlic paste
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) ginger paste
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter
  • 400g (14oz) blended fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) sugar

Spice Blend

  • 2Tbsp paprika
  • 1Tbsp chili powder
  • 1Tbsp spicy curry powder
  • 2tsp cumin
  • 1Tbsp salt Dash of black pepper

Mac N Cheese

  • 672g (24oz) shell pasta
  • 240g (1 cup) pasta water
  • 400g (1 ⅔ cups) 0% Greek yogurt
  • 120g (½ cup) 1/3 fat cream cheese
  • 227g (2 cups) shredded extra sharp cheddar cheese
  • 80g (¾ cup) shredded reduced fat mozzarella
  • 1tsp garam masala Handful of each: chopped cilantro, chopped green onions black pepper. Stir to coat evenly.

Instructions

  1. Dice chicken breast. In a skillet, sauté onions for about 8 to 10 minutes, or until browned.
  2. Transfer the chicken and onions to the slow cooker. Add tomato paste, garlic paste, ginger paste, butter, blended fire- roasted tomatoes, sugar or zero-cal sweetener, paprika, chili powder, spicy curry powder, cumin, salt, and a dash of
  3. Cook on high for 2–3 hours or low for 3–4 hours, until the chicken is tender.
  4. Meanwhile, cook shell pasta to 50% of the box time. Reserve the pasta water, then drain and set aside.
  5. Once the chicken is cooked, stir in plain 0% Greek yogurt, 1/3 fat cream cheese, a handful of chopped cilantro and a handful of chopped green onions. Mix until smooth.
  6. Add the half-cooked pasta and reserved pasta water. Stir in garam masala, shredded extra sharp cheddar, and shredded reduced fat mozzarella. Mix until evenly combined.
  7. Cover and cook on high for 15-20 minutes, until the pasta is fully cooked and the sauce is thick and creamy. Stir thoroughly. Divide into 10 portions and enjoy. Store leftovers in freezer-safe containers after cooling completely.
