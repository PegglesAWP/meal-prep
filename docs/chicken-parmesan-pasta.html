Ingredients

Chicken & Tomato Sauce

  • 1120g (40oz) chicken breast
  • 794g (28oz) can of peeled or crushed tomatoes
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) tomato paste
  • 2Tbsp minced garlic
  • 1Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1tsp black pepper
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp chili flakes
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) Parmigiano Reggiano

Cheese Sauce

  • 300g (1 ¼ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 240g (1 cup) fat-free milk
  • 100g (½ cup) 1/3-fat cream cheese
  • 150g (1 ½ cups) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) white miso (optional) Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Topping: Chopped fresh basil

Pasta

  • 672g (24oz) pasta of choice smooth.
