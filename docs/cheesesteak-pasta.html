Beef & Seasonings

  • 1350g (48oz) flat cut brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 1Tbsp each: Salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder
  • 240g (8oz) beef bone broth

Roasted Peppers

  • 4 red bell peppers
  • 4 poblanos
  • 4 jalapenos
  • 2 sweet onions

Cheese Sauce

  • 800g (3 ⅓ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 100g (3 ⅓ oz) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 150g (5 ⅓ oz) provolone
  • 350g (~1 ½ cups) 1% milk To taste: salt & pepper

Pasta

  • 672g (24oz) pasta of choice (dry weight)

Instructions

  1. Add beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and beef bone broth to a slow cooker
  2. Cook on high for 4-5 hours, or low for 5-6 hours
  3. Dice bell peppers, poblanos, jalapenos, and sweet onions, and add a sheet pan. Bake at 450 for 30-45
  4. Once beef is done cooking, shred, then mix in roasted peppers and onions.
  5. In a blender, blend 2% cottage cheese, Parmigiano Reggiano, provolone, 1% milk plus salt and pepper
  6. Cook pasta for half the time on the packaging instructions, drain.
  7. Add half-cooked pasta and the blended cheese sauce to the shredded beef and pepper mixture. Cover and let cook on low for 20-25 minutes until the pasta is finished cooking and the sauce is thickened. Portion
