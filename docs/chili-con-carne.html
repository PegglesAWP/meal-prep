Beef & Marinade

  • 2010g (72oz) flat cut brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) avocado oil
  • 2 medium yellow onions
  • 8cloves garlic
  • 60g (4 Tbsp) tomato paste
  • 355g (12oz) Modelo Negro beer
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) Worcestershire
  • 475g (16oz) beef bone broth
  • 200g (¾ cup) red enchilada sauce
  • 20g (1 Tbsp) honey
  • 200g (7oz can) chipotle peppers

Add At End

  • 1.5 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) corn starch + 2 Tbsp cold water Handful of chopped cilantro

Chili Seasoning

  • 1.5Tbsp chili powder
  • 1.5Tbsp ancho chili powder
  • 1.5Tbsp salt
  • 1.5Tbsp ground cumin
  • 1Tbsp pepper
  • 1Tbsp dried oregano

Toppings (per serving)

  • 75g (⅓ cup) 0% Greek yogurt
  • 15g (2 Tbsp) sharp cheddar Chopped cilantro Green onions

Instructions

  1. Trim flat cut brisket or chuck eye roast, of excess separable fat. Place the meat in the slow cooker.
  2. Dice onions. In a large skillet over medium heat, sauté the onions in the avocado oil for 5-7 minutes until translucent and beginning to brown. Mince the garlic and add to the skillet along with tomato paste. Stir continuously and simmer for 5–6
  3. Pour in the Modelo Negro (or substitute additional 475g/16oz beef bone broth) and bring to a boil. Reduce the liquid completely while stirring occasionally, then pour in the beef bone broth and Worcestershire sauce. Stir to combine and remove from heat. Pour the onion and broth mixture over the beef, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Add red enchilada sauce, honey, chipotle peppers, chili powder, ancho chili powder, salt, ground cumin, pepper, dried oregano, powdered cinnamon, + smoked paprika. Mix everything.
  5. Cover the slow cooker and cook on low for 4–5 hours, until the beef is tender and the flavors are well developed.
  6. Once cooking is complete, stir in apple cider vinegar and a handful of chopped cilantro. Adjust salt to taste.
  7. In a small bowl, whisk cornstarch with cold water to form a slurry. Stir gradually into the chili and cook on low until thickened
  8. Assemble into 10 even servings. Shred the cheddar cheese and top each bowl with cheddar cheese, 0% Greek yogurt, chopped cilantro, and sliced green onions. Store leftovers in freezer-safe, airtight containers.
