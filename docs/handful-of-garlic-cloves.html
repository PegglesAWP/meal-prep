Ingredients

Main

  • 3 Cheese Chicken Alfredo

Garlic Chicken

  • 900g (32oz) chicken breast
  • 1Tbsp salt
  • 2tsp garlic powder
  • 2tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp black pepper
  • 1tsp Italian seasoning
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter

Roasted Garlic

  • 5g (1 tsp) oil Pinch of salt

3 Cheese Alfredo Sauce

  • 800g (3 ½ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 120g (½ cup) 1/3 fat cream cheese
  • 120g (1 ½ cups) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 60g (¾ cup) Asiago
  • 360g (1 ½ cups) milk To taste: salt & pepper

Pasta

  • 672g (6 ¾ cups) pasta of choice
  • 120g (~½ cup) pasta water among ten freezer-safe containers.

Instructions

  1. Dice chicken breast into evenly sized chunks, then place into a slow cooker along with salt, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, Italian seasoning, and butter; mix until evenly coated.
  2. Wrap garlic cloves in foil with oil and a pinch of salt. Place the foil in the slow cooker with chicken.
  3. Cover and cook on high for 2–3 hours or on low for 3–4 hours.
  4. Cook pasta for half of the time stated on the package, reserving pasta water before draining.
  5. Blend 2% cottage cheese, 1/3 fat cream cheese, grated Parmigiano Reggiano, grated Asiago, and milk until completely smooth; season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. When the chicken is done, remove the foil packet, shred the chicken directly in the cooker, break up the roasted garlic cloves in the slow cooker, and stir to distribute.
  7. Place half-cooked pasta, reserved pasta water, and blended three-cheese Alfredo sauce in the slow cooker with the chicken. Mix well, cover again, and cook on high for 15–20 min. Divide the finished Alfredo equally
