Enchilada Sauce

  • 420g (1 ¾ cups) red enchilada sauce
  • 200g (7oz can) chipotle peppers
  • 1 white onion Juice of 1 lime
  • 1.5Tbsp salt
  • 1Tbsp garlic powder Dash of black pepper

Additional Ingredients

  • 60g (3 Tbsp) honey
  • 1bunch chopped cilantro

Instructions

  1. To a slow cooker, add chicken thighs, diced white onion, red enchilada sauce, chipotle peppers, lime juice, salt, garlic powder, and a dash of black pepper. Stir to ensure the chicken is evenly coated with the sauce and spices. Cover the slow cooker and cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 3-4 hours, until the
  2. Once the chicken is fully cooked, use two forks to shred it directly in the slow cooker, mixing it well into the sauce to absorb all the flavors. Add the honey and cilantro to the slow cooker, stirring gently to combine.
  3. Cover the slow cooker again and let it cook on high for an additional 15 minutes to allow the flavors to blend into the sauce. After cooking, stir gently but thoroughly
  4. Divide into 16 servings and serve. Store leftovers in airtight, freezer-safe containers once cooled
