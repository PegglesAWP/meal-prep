Ingredients

Buffalo Chicken

  • 1340g (48oz) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp onion powder
  • 1tsp paprika
  • 1tsp chili powder
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning
  • 1Tbsp fresh chopped dill
  • 100g (½ cup) buffalo sauce

Buffalo Cheese Sauce

  • 750g (3 ¼ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 200g (¾ cup) 1/3 fat cream cheese
  • 200g (¾ cup) buffalo sauce
  • 200g (1 ¾ cups) 2% cheddar cheese
  • 60g (3 Tbsp) honey
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter Handful of chopped chives cheese, honey, additional buffalo sauce, butter. loaded fries, or reheat as a dip.

Instructions

  1. Add chicken thighs to your slow cooker. Sprinkle with salt, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, chili powder, ranch seasoning, and fresh chopped dill.
  2. Pour in buffalo sauce and mix well to evenly coat the chicken. Cover and cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 3-4 hours, until the chicken is tender and easily shredded.
  3. Once the chicken is cooked, shred it directly in the slow cooker using two forks. Blend the 2% cottage cheese and shred the 2% cheddar cheese. Mix into the slow cooker along with the 1/3 fat cream
  4. Stir thoroughly until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Mix in chopped chives for flavor.
