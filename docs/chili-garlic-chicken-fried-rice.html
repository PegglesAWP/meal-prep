Ingredients

Chili Garlic Chicken

  • 1360g (48oz) boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 60g (4 Tbsp) chili garlic sauce
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) soy sauce
  • 60g (3 Tbsp) honey
  • 1Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1Tbsp minced ginger Salt & pepper to taste

Low-Calorie Spicy Mayo

  • 150g (⅔ cup) 0% skyr or 0% Greek yogurt
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) light mayo
  • 35g (2 Tbsp) gochujang
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) sriracha Splash of rice vinegar

Fried Rice

  • 1400g (7 cups) day-old cooked rice (600g {3 cups} uncooked weight)
  • 60g (4 Tbsp) soy sauce
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) fish sauce
  • 60g (3 Tbsp) honey
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) rice vinegar
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) sesame oil
  • 600g (4 cups) mixed frozen veggies of choice Chopped green onions
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) sesame oil (post-cook mix)

Egg Mixture

  • 400g (1 ⅔ cups) egg whites
  • 6 eggs sesame oil and chopped green onions. Stir well.

Instructions

  1. If using day-old rice, cook the rice the day before and let rest in the fridge until making this recipe.
  2. Slice the chicken thighs in half and add them to the slow cooker. Pour in chili garlic sauce, soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, minced ginger, salt, and pepper. Mix to coat evenly, cover, and cook on high for 2-3 hrs or on low for 3-4 hrs until tender. Use tongs to break up the chicken when done.
  3. Spread day-old (or pre-cooked) rice on a large sheet pan. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, fish sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and sesame oil, then pour over the rice and toss to coat.
  4. Preheat the oven to 475°F, then broil the rice for 10-25 min, checking every 5 min, until crispy and browned. Remove from oven, scrape the edges, add frozen veggies on top, and broil for another 10-15 min until veggies are cooked. Transfer rice and veggies to the slow cooker, and mix with the cooked chicken. Add egg whites and eggs to the same pan, and broil for 3-4 min or until set. Chop eggs and add to slow cooker with remaining
  5. For the spicy mayo, mix 0% skyr or Greek yogurt, light mayo, gochujang, sriracha, rice vinegar, salt, and pepper, adding water to reach your desired consistency. Drizzle or mix into the fried rice.
  6. Divide into 10 servings, allow to cool, and store leftovers in freezer-safe containers.
