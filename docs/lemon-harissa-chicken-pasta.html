Chicken

  • 1340g (48oz) boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Harissa Sauce

  • 2 yellow onions
  • 60g (4 Tbsp) harissa paste
  • 60g (4 Tbsp) sun-dried tomatoes
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) tomato paste
  • 20g (1 Tbsp) honey
  • 2tsp each: smoked paprika, dried thyme, dried oregano
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1Tbsp salt Dash of black pepper
  • Additional Ingredients: Juice and zest of 2 lemons
  • 3Tbsp harissa paste
  • 200g (1¼ cups) feta cheese
  • 500g (2 cups) whole milk

Pasta

  • 672g (24 oz) penne pasta

Lazy Tzatziki

  • 200g (¾ cup) store-bought tzatziki
  • 200g (¾ cup) 0% Greek yogurt Salt/garlic powder, to taste
  • Garnish: Chopped chives

Instructions

  1. Dice the yellow onions, chop the sun-dried tomatoes, and place in the slow cooker with the chicken thighs, harissa paste, tomato paste, honey, smoked paprika, dried thyme, dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper into a slow cooker. Mix and coat the chicken evenly. Cover and cook on high for 3–4 hrs or low for 4–5 hrs, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  2. Once the chicken is cooked, shred it directly in the slow cooker using two forks. Add the juice and zest of two lemons, additional harissa paste, and crumbled feta cheese. Stir to combine, then cover and let cook on high
  3. Cook the penne pasta until it is halfway done according to package instructions. Drain the pasta and add it to the slow cooker along with the whole milk. Stir everything together, ensuring the pasta is evenly coated. Cover and cook on low for 20–30 minutes, or until the pasta is fully cooked and the sauce has thickened.
  4. In a small bowl, mix the store-bought tzatziki with the 0% Greek yogurt. Add salt and garlic powder to taste, stirring until smooth. Divide into 10 servings. Top each pasta bowl with a dollop of tzatziki and garnish with chopped chives before serving. Store leftovers in freezer-safe containers.
