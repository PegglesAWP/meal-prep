Chicken & Marinade

  • 1340g (48oz) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 200g (¾ cup) Buffalo wing sauce*
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) soy sauce
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning
  • 40g (2 Tbsp) honey

Cornstarch Slurry

  • 3Tbsp cornstarch
  • 3Tbsp cold water

Rice

  • 240g (1 ⅓ cups) dry rice
  • 560g (2 ⅓ cups) bone broth To taste: salt + garlic powder

Buffalo Ranch Sauce

  • 75g (⅓ cup) Buffalo wing sauce Handful of chopped chives or green onions
  • 200g (¾ cup) 0% Greek yogurt
  • 75g (⅓ cup) light sour cream Pinch of chopped dill (optional)

Toppings & Garnish

  • 120g (4 oz) mozzarella
  • 70g (2 ½ oz) applewood smoked bacon bits (buy pre-cooked bits) Chopped chives
