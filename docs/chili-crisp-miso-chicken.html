Ingredients

Main

  • and cooked through. containers

Chicken

  • 1340g (48oz) boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Miso Sauce

  • 60g (4 Tbsp) white miso paste
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) chili crisp
  • 40g (2 Tbsp) honey
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) mirin
  • 1Tbsp garlic powder To taste: black pepper and salt

Onions

  • 1 white onion
  • Toppings: White/black sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together white miso paste, chili crisp, honey, mirin, garlic powder, black pepper and salt until combined. Add chicken thighs to the slow cooker, and pour the miso sauce over the chicken, stirring to coat evenly. Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours or low for 4-5 hours, until the chicken is tender
  2. Cook 1 white onion over medium heat in a pan until translucent and beginning to brown
  3. Once cooking is done, shred the cooked chicken using two forks, leaving larger pieces intact for texture. Mix in cooked onions. Top with white & black sesame seeds in the slow cooker.
  4. Mix until all ingredients are fully combined. Divide into 12 servings and serve. Store leftovers in freezer-safe
