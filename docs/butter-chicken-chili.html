Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1350g (48oz) ground chicken thighs

Spice blend

  • 3Tbsp smoked paprika
  • 2Tbsp cumin
  • 1Tbsp garam masala
  • 2tsp coriander
  • 1tsp cayenne
  • 1Tbsp salt Dash of black pepper

Toppings & Garnish

  • 300g (1 ¼ cups) plain 0% Greek yogurt or skyr
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter
  • ½ spice blend Cilantro for garnish

Butter Sauce

  • 45g (3 Tbsp) butter
  • 3 onions
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) tomato paste
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) garlic paste
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) ginger paste
  • ½ spice blend
  • 794g (28oz) crushed fire-roasted tomatoes (can)
  • 240g (1 cup) chicken bone broth
  • 20g (1 Tbsp) honey

Lemon Yogurt Sauce (optional)

  • 100g (½ cup) Greek yogurt Juice of ¼ lemon Salt & garlic to taste taste after reheating.

Instructions

  1. Combine smoked paprika, cumin, garam masala, coriander, cayenne, salt, and black pepper
  2. Prep the butter sauce. Dice onions and cook over high heat until translucent and starting to brown. Reduce heat to medium, add butter, and simmer for 5-10 min, stirring occasionally. Mix in tomato paste, garlic paste, ginger paste, and half the spice blend, cooking for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed fire-roasted tomatoes, chicken bone broth, and honey. Mix well, simmer briefly, then remove from heat and let cool slightly before blending until smooth.
  4. Place ground chicken in a slow cooker and pour the blended sauce over it. Stir to coat, cover, and cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 3-4 hours. Break up large chunks of chicken until it reaches a chili-like consistency. Stir in 0% Greek yogurt (or skyr), butter, the remaining spice blend, and chopped cilantro until fully combined. Divide into 7 portions for meal prep and let leftovers cool before storing in freezer-safe containers.
