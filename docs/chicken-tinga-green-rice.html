Ingredients

Meat

  • 670g (24oz) skinless chicken thighs
  • 670g (24oz) chicken breasts

Tinga Sauce

  • 45g (3 Tbsp) olive oil

  • 794g (28oz) fire-roasted diced tomatoes
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) apple cider vinegar

  • 3 bay leaves

Topping

  • 50g (⅓ cup) Cotija cheese Chopped cilantro leaves

Spice Blend

  • 4tsp oregano
  • 1tsp smoked paprika
  • 0.5tsp cumin
