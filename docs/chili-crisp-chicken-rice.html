Chili Crisp Chicken & Rice

Ingredients

Chili Crisp Chicken

  • 1360g (48oz) chicken breast
  • 100g (7 Tbsp) chili crisp
  • 80g (4 Tbsp) honey
  • 60g (4 Tbsp) soy sauce
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) sriracha Season to taste with: Salt, pepper, garlic powder

Rice

  • 480g (2 ½ cups) short grain or sushi rice
  • 720g (3 cups) chicken bone broth

Spicy Mayo

  • 50g (3 Tbsp) light mayo
  • 150g (⅔ cup) 0% Greek yogurt
  • 70g (~5 Tbsp) sriracha
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) no-sugar ketchup Season to taste with: salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • 1% milk, as needed for consistency

Garnish

  • 30g (2 Tbsp) rice vinegar
  • 2 chopped green onions Sesame seeds and garlic powder. Stir to coat the chicken. and sesame seeds to taste.

Instructions

  1. Place chicken breast in a slow cooker. Add chili crisp, soy sauce, sriracha, honey, and a pinch of salt, pepper,
  2. Cover and cook on high for 3–4 hours or low for 4–5 hours until the chicken is fully cooked.
  3. While the chicken cooks, add rice and chicken bone broth to a pot. Bring to a boil and reduce to a low simmer. Cover and cook for 15–18 minutes. Let rest for 5 minutes then fluff with a fork.
  4. Shred the cooked chicken directly in the slow cooker using two forks. Stir in rice vinegar, chopped green onions,
  5. Add the cooked rice to the slow cooker and mix thoroughly until combined.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together light mayo, 0% Greek yogurt, sriracha, and no-sugar ketchup. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Stir in splashes of 1% milk until desired consistency is reached.
  7. Divide the chicken and rice mixture evenly into 8 servings. Drizzle each with spicy mayo. Enjoy immediately, let leftovers cool before freezing, then store in freezer-safe meal prep containers.
