Instructions

  1. In a slow cooker, add chicken thighs, taco seasoning, red enchilada sauce, fire-roasted frozen corn, black beans, and salt to taste. Mix until ingredients are fully combined. Cover and cook on high for 2–3 hours or low for 3–4 hours, until the chicken is tender and fully cooked through.
  2. When the chicken is nearly ready, in a medium pot, combine jasmine rice and chicken bone broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover for ~20 min. Let it rest, covered, for 5 min. Fluff with a fork.
