Thai red Curry Peanut Chicken

Ingredients

Meat

  • 1790g (64oz) boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Red Curry Peanut Sauce

  • 120g (½ Cup) peanut butter
  • 100g (⅓ Cup) Thai red curry paste
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) soy sauce
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) fish sauce
  • 50g (2.5 Tbsp) honey
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) grated ginger
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) grated garlic Juice + zest of 1 lime
  • 160g (⅔ cup) coconut cream
  • Toppings: Juice of 1-2 limes (to taste) Chopped Thai basil (handful)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together peanut butter, Thai red curry paste, soy sauce, fish sauce, honey, ginger, garlic, lime juice, zest, and coconut cream until smooth. Divide the sauce in half.
  2. Place chicken thighs in the slow cooker and pour half the sauce over them. Mix to coat evenly. Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours or low for 4-5 hours, until tender.
  3. Once the chicken is done, shred lightly or leave in chunks. Add the remaining sauce and juice of 1-2 limes (to taste). Stir well, add chopped Thai basil and let sit for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Divide into 16 servings, storing leftovers in freezer-safe containers for meal prep.
