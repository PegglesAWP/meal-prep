  • 1350g (48oz) boneless, skinless chicken thighs

Base Ingredients

  • 40g (2 Tbsp) honey

Curry Roux & Flavorings

  • 10 Golden Curry cubes (Japanese curry roux)
  • 200g (¾ cup) applesauce or 2 grated apples

Rice

  • 500g (2½ cups) short-grain rice

Instructions

  1. Dice the chicken thighs, carrots, Yukon gold potatoes, and onions into bite sized chunks. Pour in the beef bone broth, Worcestershire sauce, and honey. Stir everything to combine, ensuring the chicken and vegetables are coated in the liquid. Cover the slow cooker and cook on high for 4–5 hours or low for 5–6 hours, until the chicken is tender and the vegetables are cooked through.
  2. Once the cooking time is complete, break apart the Golden Curry cubes (Japanese curry roux cubes) into small pieces. Add the roux cubes to the slow cooker one at a time, stirring thoroughly after each addition to ensure they dissolve completely into the liquid.
