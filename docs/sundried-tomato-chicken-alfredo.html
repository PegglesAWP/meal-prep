Ingredients

Tomato Chicken

  • 900g (32oz) chicken breast
  • 794g (28oz can) San Marzano tomatoes
  • 85g (~⅓ cup) chopped sundried tomatoes
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) olive oil
  • 1Tbsp salt
  • 1Tbsp dried oregano
  • 1Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1Tbsp red chili flakes
  • 1tsp black pepper
  • 1 parmesan rind (optional)

High-Protein Alfredo Sauce

  • 800g (3 ⅓ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 120g (4oz) ⅓-fat cream cheese
  • 120g (4oz) grated Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 360g (1 ½ cups) fat-free milk To taste: salt and pepper

Pasta

  • 672g (24oz) linguine Optionally, nestle in a parmesan rind.
  • 20 minutes, until the sauce thickens and the pasta finishes cooking.

Instructions

  1. Add diced chicken breast, San Marzano tomatoes, chopped sundried tomatoes, olive oil, salt, dried oregano, garlic powder, red chili flakes, and black pepper to the slow cooker. Stir to combine.
  2. Cover and cook on high for 2–3 hrs or low for 3–4 hrs, until chicken is fully cooked and tender.
  3. While chicken finishes, cook linguine to half the time listed on the box. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a blender, combine 2% cottage cheese, ⅓-fat cream cheese, grated Parmigiano Reggiano, and fat- free milk. Blend until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Remove the parmesan rind (if used). Shred chicken in the slow cooker with two forks. Add the half-cooked pasta and pour in the blended Alfredo sauce . Stir to combine thoroughly. Cover and cook on high for 15–
  6. Divide into 10 equal meal prep containers. Let cool and store leftovers in freezer safe containers.
