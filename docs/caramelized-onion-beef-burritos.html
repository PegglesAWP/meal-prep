Caramelized onion beef Burritos

Ingredients

Beef & Seasoning

  • 1340g (48oz) flat cut brisket or chuck eye roast
  • 45g (3 Tbsp) Worcestershire
  • 15g (1 Tbsp) Dijon mustard
  • 1Tbsp salt
  • 1Tbsp garlic powder
  • 2tsp onion powder
  • 2tsp black pepper
  • 1tsp dried thyme
  • 0.125tsp nutmeg
  • 100g (½ cup) bone broth

Tortillas

  • 11 burrito tortillas

Caramelized Onions

  • 4 yellow onions
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) butter
  • 960g (32oz) beef bone broth
  • 30g (2 Tbsp) Worcestershire Sprig of rosemary and thyme

Cheesy Rice

  • 240g (1 ¼ cups) rice
  • 300g (1 ¼ cups) 0% Greek yogurt
  • 60g (½ cup) cheddar cheese
  • 1tsp nutritional yeast (optional) Cilantro or green onions

Instructions

  1. Trim brisket or chuck eye roast of excess separable fat. Place in the slow cooker and add Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, dried thyme, nutmeg, and beef bone broth. Stir to coat evenly. Cover and cook on high for 4–5 hours or low for 5–6 hours, until tender.
  2. While the beef cooks, prep the caramelized onions. Dice the onions and heat a large skillet over high heat. Cook until translucent and turning brown. Reduce the heat to medium, add the butter, and stir to coat the onions. Continue to sauté for 10–15 min, stirring often, until the onions are deeply browned and caramelized.
  3. Pour in the beef bone broth and Worcestershire, stirring to combine. Optionally, add a sprig of rosemary and thyme for extra flavor. Bring the mixture to a boil and cook until the liquid has fully evaporated, leaving richly flavored onions. Remove and discard the rosemary and thyme, if used.
  4. Once the beef is fully cooked, shred it directly in the slow cooker using two forks. Add the caramelized onions to the slow cooker and stir well to combine with the beef and its juices.
  5. Steam cook the rice according to package instructions. Fluff with a fork, then shred the cheddar cheese and stir in the slow cooker with the nutritional yeast (if using), 0% yogurt, chopped cilantro or green onions, and season with salt and garlic powder to taste. Mix well to create the cheesy rice.
  6. Lay out the 11 tortillas and add 1–2 spoonfuls of cheesy rice to each. Top with the shredded beef and caramelized onion mixture, then roll each tortilla tightly into a burrito.
