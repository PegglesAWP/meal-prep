Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix coarse salt, onion powder, garlic powder, black pepper, oregano, cumin, diced tomatoes, sweet relish, tomato paste, garlic purée, and diced chipotles until combined.
  2. Dice center-cut bacon. Place 93% ground beef in the slow cooker, pour in half of the marinade, and line the sides with the bacon pieces. Cook on low for 3–4 hours or high for 2–3 hours until the beef is tender.

  4. Blend 2% cottage cheese, sharp cheddar, American cheese, and 1% milk. Add salt + pepper to taste.
