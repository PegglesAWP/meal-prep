Ingredients

Chicken & Bacon

  • 896g (32oz) chicken breast
  • 6 slices turkey bacon
  • 1 packet ranch seasoning
  • 30g (1 ⅓ Tbsp) hot honey
  • 1Tbsp fresh dill Garlic powder, black pepper, & red chili flakes, to taste

Broth Base

  • 3 sweet onions
  • 6 jalapeños or Fresno chilis
  • 8cloves of garlic
  • 480g (16oz) chicken bone broth

Cheese Sauce

  • 800g (3 ⅓ cups) 2% cottage cheese
  • 160g (1 ¼ cups) Parmigiano Reggiano
  • 420g (1 ¾ cups) fat-free milk
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp onion powder Salt & pepper, to taste

Pasta

  • 672g (24 oz) pasta of choice

Topping & Garnish

  • 10 slices center-cut bacon handful of chopped chives completely. Set aside. pasta is tender and sauce thickens.

Instructions

  1. Mince the garlic and set aside. Dice the onions and brown in a large skillet. Once browned, add jalapeños (or Fresno chilis) and garlic, cooking until softened. Pour in chicken bone broth, bring to a boil, and reduce liquid
  2. Dice the chicken breast and chop the dill. Then combine these in slow cooker with diced turkey bacon, ranch seasoning, hot honey, garlic powder, black pepper, and red chili flakes. Pour in the onion, garlic and broth base, stir, and cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 3-4 hours, until chicken is tender. Lightly shred with tongs.
  3. Boil pasta in lightly salted water until it’s half cooked according to the package instructions.
  4. For the cheese sauce, In a blender, add Parmigiano Reggiano, 2% cottage cheese, fat-free milk, garlic powder, and onion powder. Add salt and pepper to taste and blend until smooth.   5. Meanwhile, bake center-cut bacon at 400°F for 15-20 minutes until crispy. Chop then mix into slow cooker. Garnish with chives, split into 11 servings. Store leftovers in freezer-safe containers.
