Instructions

  1. Grate the ginger and mix in a large bowl, whisk together sugar-free BBQ sauce, soy sauce, fish sauce, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, and black pepper until smooth. Cut the fresh pineapple into bite- sized chunks. Place the chicken thighs and pineapple chunks into the slow cooker. Pour the prepared marinade over the chicken and pineapple, stirring to coat evenly. Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours or low for 4-5 hours, until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
